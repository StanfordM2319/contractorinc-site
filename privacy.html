Privacy Policy
Effective date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: 2026
⚡ Short version — plain English
  • We collect zero personal data. None.
  • All your game saves live on your device only.
  • No accounts, no sign-ins, no tracking, no ads.
  • The optional AI feature talks directly to Anthropic — we never see your key.
1. Who we are

Contractor Inc is a skilled trades business simulation game developed by Stanford Inc ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data is collected when you use the app and how it is handled.

2. Data we collect

We collect no personal data. Contractor Inc does not require an account, does not collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, or any personally identifiable information.

All game progress — your company name, financial records, crew, and game settings — is stored entirely on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server operated by us.

3. Local storage
Game data is saved to your device under the keys ec_save_0 through ec_save_4 (up to 5 save slots). You can delete all of this data at any time by tapping Reset All Data in the app's Settings tab, or by clearing your browser/app storage.
4. Optional AI features

Contractor Inc optionally supports AI-enriched job descriptions. This feature is entirely optional and disabled by default. If you choose to enable it, you supply your own Anthropic API key. That key is:

Stored only on your device in localStorage.
Never transmitted to us.
Sent only to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com) directly from your device when you use the feature.

Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle API requests.
5. Google Fonts

The app loads typefaces from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) when an internet connection is available. This is a standard web font service. If you are offline, system fonts are used automatically and no request is made. Google's privacy policy governs their font service.

6. Third-party services

Contractor Inc does not use any analytics services, advertising networks, social login SDKs, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party data collection services.

The only external network requests the app makes are: Google Fonts (optional, for typography) and Anthropic API (optional, only if you supply an API key).

7. Children's privacy

Contractor Inc is rated for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children or any users, as no data collection occurs at all. Parents and guardians can be assured that the app does not communicate with any server that collects personal information.

8. Your rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. All data is on your device and under your control. To delete your game data, use Settings → Reset All Data inside the app.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? You can reach Stanford Inc through the contact information listed on our app store page. We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.
