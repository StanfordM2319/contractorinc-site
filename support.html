Frequently Asked Questions
How do I save my game? ›
Your game auto-saves every time you advance a week and whenever you leave the app. You can also manually save at any time by going to Settings → Save Game. Up to 5 separate save slots are available.
I lost my save / my save is gone. What happened? ›
Save data is stored in your device's local storage. It can be lost if you: clear your browser/app cache, uninstall and reinstall the app, or reset your device without a backup. We recommend using Settings → Save Slots to keep multiple saves as a backup. Unfortunately, if local storage is cleared, the data cannot be recovered.
What is the AI feature and do I need it? ›
The AI feature generates unique client quotes, site notes, and competitor intel for each job RFP. It's completely optional — the game is fully playable without it. To enable it, you need your own Anthropic API key (from console.anthropic.com). Your key is stored only on your device and costs are billed directly by Anthropic — typically a few cents per game session.
How do I get an Anthropic API key? ›
Go to console.anthropic.com, create a free account, and generate an API key. Keys start with sk-ant-. Paste it into the API key field in the app. Note: API usage is billed by Anthropic — check their pricing page for current rates.
How do I start my first job? ›
1. Go to the Jobs tab and tap an available RFP. 2. Enter your bid amount and tap Submit Bid. 3. Advance the week — if you win, the job moves to Active. 4. Assign workers to the job from the Active Jobs panel. 5. Advance weeks to complete it and collect payment.
My reputation keeps dropping. Why? ›
Reputation drops when: jobs are completed late, clients leave bad reviews, you miss payroll, or you default on a loan. Keep jobs staffed with enough workers, don't over-bid your capacity, and pay your crew on time. Marketing campaigns (Marketing tab) can help recover reputation faster.
How do union contracts work? ›
Union contracts are negotiated every few seasons. When your CBA comes up for renewal, you'll receive a weekly event card to negotiate wages, benefits, and work rules. Offering fair wages avoids strikes. You can view your current contract in the Corporate tab under Union Management.
What does the Fab Shop do? ›
The Fabrication Shop lets you build custom steel components, pipe spools, and process equipment in-house. Once built, FAB SHOP RFPs appear on your job board — these are higher-value contracts. You can upgrade it in 3 tiers from the Finance tab → Fab Shop card.
How do I delete all my data? ›
Go to Settings → ⚠️ Danger Zone → Reset All Data. This permanently wipes all save slots and settings from your device. This cannot be undone.
The app seems slow or is not loading. What can I do? ›
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, check that your device has available storage. The game file is large (~3MB) and requires a recent WebView version. On Android, make sure Android System WebView is up to date via the Play Store.
Pro Tips
💡 Cash flow is everything. Never let your cash drop below one full week of payroll. Take a short-term loan before you miss payroll — missing it tanks your reputation and morale fast.

💡 Hire a Foreman early. Foremen boost crew efficiency and unlock larger job crews, which means bigger jobs and higher revenue per week.

💡 Watch your bid margin. Set a minimum profit margin in Settings → Bidding. Winning jobs at a loss is worse than not winning them at all.
